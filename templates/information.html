Constant :



Laplace Transform of Constant requires only one value.

Therefore,

Input1 = Constant Value,

Input2 = 0.

Exponential :



Laplace Transform of Exponential Function requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = Constant Value,

Input2 = α.

Sine :



Laplace Transform of Sine Function requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = Constant Value,

Input2 = ω.

Cosine :




Laplace Transform of Cosine Function requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = Constant Value,

Input2 = ω.

T :



Laplace Transform of T Function requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = Constant Value,

Input2 = n.

Exponential-Cosine (First Shifting of Cosine) :



The first shifting of Cosine requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = α,

Input2 = ω.

Exponential-Sine (First Shifting of Sine) :




The first shifting of Sine requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = α,

Input2 = ω.

Exponential-T (First Shifting of T) :




The first shifting of T requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = n,

Input2 = α.

Sin h :




Laplace Transform of Sin h Function requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = Constant Value,

Input2 = α.

Cos h :




Laplace Transform of Cos h Function requires two inputs.

Therefore,

Input1 = Constant Value,

Input2 = α.
